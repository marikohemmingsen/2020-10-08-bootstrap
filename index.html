Why us?

Wellington is the city that has it all. New Zealand’s capital city regularly comes out in the top five of the Most Liveable City rankings, claiming the top spot in 2017 and 2018. Contributing to this achievement is the efficient public transport, numerous parks, minimal pollution and crime, value for money and its array of outstanding cafés.

Our products

Square Plates

Clean, crisp white porcelain dinnerware combines squares and curves in a dynamic contemporary design. Our durable dinner plate, a Crate and Barrel exclusive, resists chips and stains and is safe to use in the dishwasher, microwave and oven up to 350°F.

Cappuccino Cups

Enjoy your frothy "caffè" favorite in our classic porcelain cup with simple coffee-bar styling and matching saucer. The wide cup presents cappuccinos, hot chocolate and other foam-topped beverages beautifully, and the graceful handle protects hands from hot drinks.

Round Plates

Our contemporary porcelain dinnerware sets a table of refinement and rusticity. Low-profile appetizer plates take on organic overtones with freeform rims and a finely "hammered" surface that ripples the light.
